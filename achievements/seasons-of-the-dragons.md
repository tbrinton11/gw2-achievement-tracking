## Sub-Achievement Checklist

Ordered from easiest to hardest. Each "Return to" meta-achievement requires replaying the episode's story, completing map events, and doing episode-specific objectives.

---

S2 maps (Dry Top, Silverwastes) are compact. Story instances are short. Events are straightforward and soloable.

## Strategy Notes

- **Start with Season 2** — shortest episodes, smallest maps, most can be done in 30-60 min each
- **Season 3 episodes** can be done alongside gizmo vendor purchases (buy portal scrolls while you're on the map)
- **Season 4** — you already have Domain of Istan and Sandswept Isles done, so 4 remaining
- **IBS** — save for last, Drizzlewood Coast meta doubles as Significant Otter farming (Memories of Otter drop there)
- Each Return meta-achievement requires the corresponding Living World episode unlocked
