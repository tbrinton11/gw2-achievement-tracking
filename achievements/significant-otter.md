## Prerequisites Checklist

Must be completed in order before Memories of Otter will drop:

- [ ] **Olaf Olafson's Secret** — Hidden achievement in Bjora Marches
  - Find Olaf Olafson's hidden cave in Bjora Marches
  - Interact with objects inside the cave
- [ ] **Treasure Sleuth** — Collection achievement
  - Follow treasure clues across Bjora Marches
  - Unlocked after completing Olaf Olafson's Secret
- [ ] **Open the Vault** — Claw's Roost Glorious Chest
  - Open the vault with the Glorious Chest at Claw's Roost, Bjora Marches
  - Requires completing prior steps

Once all prerequisites are done, Memories of Otter will start dropping from Drizzlewood Coast content.

## Farming Strategy

### Primary: Drizzlewood Coast Meta Event

The full Drizzlewood Coast meta is the most efficient source of Memories of Otter.

**Expected drops per full meta run:**
- Tribune's Chest: ~1.2 Memories average
- Magnificent Chest: ~6 Memories average
- Other event chests throughout: ~4-5 Memories
- **Total per run: ~12 Memories of Otter**

**Runs needed:**
| Target | Memories | Estimated Runs |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 40 | ~4 runs |
| Tier 2 | 80 | ~7 runs |
| Tier 3 | 160 | ~14 runs |
| Tier 4 | 320 | ~27 runs |

**Meta event duration:** 1-2 hours per full run (north + south lanes)

**Tips:**
- Join a Drizzlewood Coast squad via LFG for organized runs
- Run during peak hours (NA evening / EU afternoon) for faster completion
- The meta runs continuously — if you arrive mid-meta you can still participate and get chests
- South meta (Jormag Rising) gives better chests than north (No Quarter)

### Alternative: Salvaging Charged Stormcaller Weapons

- Charged Stormcaller weapons can be salvaged for Memories of Otter
- These weapons drop from Drizzlewood Coast events or can be bought on the Trading Post
- Less efficient than farming the meta directly unless prices are low
- Check TP prices vs. time spent farming to decide

## Synergy with Other Tracked Achievements

- **Seasons of the Dragons:** The "Return to Drizzlewood Coast" episode is one of the 24 Return meta-achievements. Doing it while farming Memories kills two birds with one stone.
- **Teleportation Gizmos:** Buy the Drizzlewood Coast Portal Scroll (Bit 30) from Annona Vinedrummer at Umbral Grotto while you're farming.
- **Recommendation:** Do the Significant Otter prerequisites first, then farm Drizzlewood Coast for Memories while also completing the Return to Drizzlewood Coast objectives and buying the portal scroll.
